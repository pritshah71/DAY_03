4.3 The “Big Five” Verbs of Data Wrangling Data wrangling can involve a lot of different steps and operations to get data into a tidy format and ready for analysis and visualization. The vast majority of these fall under the umbrella one the following five operations:

Select columns/variables of interest

Filter rows/observations of interest

Arrange the rows of a dataset by column(s) of interest (i.e. order or sort)

Mutate the columns of a dataset (i.e. create or transform variables)

Summarize the rows of a dataset for column(s) of interest bold text
